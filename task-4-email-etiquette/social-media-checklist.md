# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
### Digital Ambassador Guide for College Students

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Enable Privacy Settings:** Regularly audit who can see your personal posts and tagged photos. | **Avoid Over-sharing:** Don't post sensitive data like your home address or phone number. |
| **Think Before You Post:** Consider if a future employer or professor would find your content appropriate. | **Never Share Credentials:** Don't post or message your passwords, OTPs, or financial details. |
| **Fact-Check Content:** Verify the authenticity of news or links before sharing them with your network. | **Don't Engage in Cyberbullying:** Avoid participating in online arguments or harassing others. |
| **Maintain Professionalism:** Use a clear, professional headshot for platforms like LinkedIn. | **Don't Click Suspicious Links:** Avoid "too good to be true" offers or unknown links sent via DM. |
| **Give Credit:** Always cite or tag the original creator when sharing someone else's work. | **Don't Post While Emotional:** Avoid posting rants that could damage your digital reputation. |
