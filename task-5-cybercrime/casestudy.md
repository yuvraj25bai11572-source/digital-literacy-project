# 🚨 TASK 5: CYBERCRIME CASE STUDY
## ⚖️ Topic: Cyberbullying 

---

### **Overview**
Cyberbullying involves using digital platforms to harass, threaten, or humiliate an individual[cite: 78]. It is a significant threat in the digital age, especially within academic communities[cite: 75, 76].

### **The Scenario**
A first-year student at a university becomes the target of an anonymous "confessions" page on Instagram. An unknown user posts edited, embarrassing photos of the student alongside false rumors regarding their academic integrity. Within hours, the post is shared across several departmental WhatsApp groups. The student begins receiving hurtful anonymous messages and feels isolated from their peers, leading to severe anxiety and a drop in class attendance.

### **How It Happens (Step-by-Step)**

1.  **Targeting:** The perpetrator identifies a victim and gathers personal photos or sensitive information[cite: 79].
2.  **Dissemination:** Harassing content is posted on public social media or shared in private group chats to maximize reach and impact[cite: 79].
3.  **Escalation:** Other users may join in by "liking," commenting, or re-sharing the content, creating a harmful "dogpiling" effect[cite: 79].

### **Target & Consequences**
* **Target:** While anyone can be targeted, students are particularly vulnerable due to high social media activity[cite: 79].
* **Consequences:** These include long-term psychological distress, significant damage to one's digital reputation, and the creation of a hostile learning environment[cite: 79].
