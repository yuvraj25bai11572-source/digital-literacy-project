# 🛡️ STAY SAFE ONLINE: PREVENTION CHECKLIST
### Digital Literacy Ambassador Guide for Students in India

---

### **Core Safety Practices**

1. **Strict Privacy:** Set all social media profiles to "Private" and only accept requests from people you know personally.
2. **Think Before Posting:** Never post highly personal or emotional content that could be used against you later.
3. **Document Evidence:** If bullied, do not delete the messages; take screenshots as proof of the harassment.
4. **Block and Report:** Immediately use the "Block" and "Report" features provided by platforms like Instagram, X, or WhatsApp.
5. **Use 2FA:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your email and social media to prevent account hijacking.
6. **Avoid Public Wi-Fi:** Do not log into sensitive accounts or perform bank transfers while connected to open college or café Wi-Fi.

### **Financial & UPI Safety**

7. **UPI Safety:** Never share your UPI PIN or scan a QR code sent by an unknown person claiming to "refund" money.
8. **Secure Transactions:** Only use trusted, official apps for financial transactions and avoid saving card details on public or shared devices.

---

### **🚨 Official Reporting Channels**

If you or someone you know is a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately:

* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline Number:** **1930** (Available 24x7)
